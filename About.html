Who Are We

Winners of international cooking competitions, James and Steve are the co-founders and head chefs at Steak House. We are experienced and passionate Chefs wanting to serve you the best delicacies that will satisfy your taste buds

Our History

Most loved steak house since 1985

Our steak story dates back to 1985 when James, a steak lover himself, had the urge of serving most delicious food for foodies like him. It was then that he met Steve, a passionate chef that had simillar intrests.


They started a small food let that made deliveries only. Seeing the love the city had for steaks, they expanded their team with 8 more chefs from different parts of the country so we could serve more varieties on the table


Ever since, we've been known as the best steak house in town serving over 1000 customers a day. Humbled by the love we've recieved, we strive to continue giving you the best
